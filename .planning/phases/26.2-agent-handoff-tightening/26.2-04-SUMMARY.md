## Decisions Made

- Gateway protocol injected LAST in contextParts chain — ensures it acts as a behavioral overlay on top of all other context (tier, RPEV, domains, skills)

- Injection is non-blocking: try/catch emits a stderr warning and continues if file missing or unreadable
